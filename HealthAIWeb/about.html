About Us

Welcome to HealthAI, where we bridge the gap between healthcare and technology to create a healthier tomorrow.

Our Mission

At HealthAI, our mission is to empower individuals and healthcare professionals through accurate, AI-driven health assessments, enabling early detection and proactive management of life-threatening conditions. Our focus revolves around heart disease, lung cancer, and colon cancer - striving to reduce the mortality rate through early intervention.

Our Journey

Founded in 2023 by a group of passionate healthcare professionals and tech enthusiasts, HealthAI has grown into a reputable platform known for its innovative approach towards predictive healthcare management. Through utilizing cutting-edge AI technology, we've developed a suite of tools that process a vast amount of health data to provide insightful health predictions.

Our Technology

We leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Large Language Models to create a reliable health risk assessment system. Our bespoke software, HealthAI-Predict, utilizes internationally available medical datasets to calculate the likelihood of serious medical conditions, helping both individuals and medical professionals make informed decisions.
